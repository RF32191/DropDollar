## SECTION 1: INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

**Registration and Account Information:**
- Email address
- Phone number
- Username
- Password (stored in encrypted form)
- Date of birth (for age verification)

**Identity Verification (for withdrawals):**
- Government-issued photo identification
- Proof of address documents
- Selfie for facial verification
- Tax identification number (W-9 for U.S. persons)

**Payment Information:**
- Billing address
- Payment card information (processed and stored by Stripe, our payment processor)
- Bank account information (for withdrawals)

**Profile Information:**
- Display name
- Avatar/profile picture
- Bio or description

**Communications:**
- Customer support inquiries
- Messages to other users
- Feedback and reviews

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access the Platform, we automatically collect:

**Device and Technical Information:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device identifiers
- Screen resolution
- Time zone

**Usage Information:**
- Pages and features visited
- Games played and scores
- Time spent on the Platform
- Click patterns and navigation
- Referring websites

**Location Information:**
- Approximate location derived from IP address
- State/country for legal compliance

**Game Data:**
- Scores, reaction times, accuracy
- Game session recordings
- Competition history
- Entry and withdrawal transactions

### 1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:
- Maintain your session and remember your preferences
- Analyze Platform usage and performance
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Improve user experience

**Types of Cookies:**
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for Platform functionality
- **Performance Cookies:** Help us understand usage patterns
- **Functional Cookies:** Remember your preferences
- **Security Cookies:** Detect fraud and protect your account

### 1.4 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction confirmations from Stripe
- **Identity Verification Services:** Verification results
- **Social Login Providers:** If you use Google, Apple, or other social logins
- **Advertising Partners:** Aggregate analytics data
